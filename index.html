When working with me You always get solutions which apply to three ground rules:

responsivity

During our "era" of creating the web, responsive design has escalated to whole different level than what we ever knew before. We have tons of new devices coming out every year which are giving us amazing and new technological possibilities of perceiving the internet itself. That's why I think responsive design is extremaly important to be able to deliver the best solutions to the clients and make Your projects work flawless every time in every conitions. Always.

clean code

As much as I'm still learning, I'm doing my best to make sure that every line of the written code is necessary, clean and work according to it's basic principal. By doing that, I can be sure that recently choosen solution will be well thought through and adding any features or neccesary changes in the future will be as fast and easy as possible. #sayNoToLegacyCode ;)

unique design

I consider design as an essential factor, when it comes to perception of Your work. Website that has an interesting, unique look and well thought through interface is getting noticed and remembered easier - therefore, it influence directly on how the company or subject You want to talk about is perceived by a viewer. I'm always paying great attention on small details and overall look to make sure that what we want to show together, will be presented in the best possible way.
